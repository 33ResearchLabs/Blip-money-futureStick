About P2P & Crypto-to-Cash Volumes

Why no live P2P chart? Major P2P venues (Binance P2P, OKX P2P, Bybit P2P, Paxful, LocalBitcoins-era successors) do not expose public volume APIs. Off-ramp/on-ramp providers (MoonPay, Ramp, Transak) also keep volumes private. What we can observe as proxies for off-ramp / cash-settlement demand:

  • USDT supply on Tron — the dominant rail for remittance & P2P in LATAM, Africa, SE Asia. Rising supply ≈ rising off-ramp demand.
  • Peg deviation — USDT trading below $1 on specific corridors (e.g. Nigeria, Argentina) signals heavy cash-out pressure.
  • Stablecoin mcap growth during BTC drawdowns — flight to cash that often routes through P2P.
  • Public reports: Chainalysis "Geography of Crypto" (annual), a16z "State of Crypto", Kaiko research, Visa stablecoin dashboard (visaonchainanalytics.com).
