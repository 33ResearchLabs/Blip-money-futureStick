Blip Money — Global Peer-to-Peer Crypto Settlement

Blip Money is a peer-to-peer crypto trading platform that lets you buy and sell USDT (and other stablecoins) directly with verified merchants. Every trade is protected by an on-chain escrow, ensuring your funds are safe until both sides confirm the transaction. Merchants compete to offer the best rates, so you always get a fair deal.

What you can do with Blip

  • Send money internationally using USDT stablecoins on the Solana network
  • Buy USDT from verified local merchants at competitive rates
  • Sell USDT and receive local currency directly in your bank account
  • Track live exchange rates across 150+ countries
  • Sign in with Google to manage your account and transaction history

Blip uses Google Sign-In to create and authenticate user accounts. Your Google account email is used only to identify your Blip account — we do not access your Gmail, contacts, or any other Google data.
